Secure offchain market platform for digital asset liquidity management, OTC trading, and asset distribution.
Offchain Market
Official repository for the Offchain Market platform, digital asset liquidity management, and OTC trading operations.

📄 Project Description
Offchain Market is a digital platform designed to facilitate over-the-counter (OTC) digital asset trading and support liquidity management for key tokens on the BNB Smart Chain. This repository contains the documentation, supply structures, and asset information managed within the ecosystem.

🔗 Asset Contract Information
This project manages and supports various digital assets, including:

⚙️ Features and Asset Management
Asset Distribution: Transparent management of the total supply of digital assets.

Liquidity and Offerings: Liquidity provision and asset parity maintenance against major stablecoins (USDT).

Burn Mechanism: Guidelines for maintaining supply balance by transferring tokens to the designated Dead Address (0x000000000000000000000000000000000000dEaD).
